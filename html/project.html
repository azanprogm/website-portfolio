What is a Portfolio?

  • Definition of Portfolio: A portfolio is a document that contains information about a person's work, achievements, training, skills, and work experience. In general, a portfolio serves to demonstrate a person's abilities, experience, and work in the context of work, education, or other fields.
  • Portfolio function:
    Documenting work > Portfolios can be used to store and show work that has been created.
    Reflecting achievement > A portfolio can be proof of the achievements and work results that have been achieved.
    present information > A portfolio can provide complete information about work experience, best work results, or best achievements ever achieved.
    reflect yourself > A portfolio can be a way to showcase one's personality and work ethic.

With a good portfolio, one can demonstrate their skills and experience effectively and increase the chances of achieving their desired goals.
